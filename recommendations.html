Recommendation Letter

"Akhyar Ahmed started working as a student at the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence in October 2020. Since then, he has made significant contributions to various projects. His notable achievements include implementing a tool for accurate translations in the SIM3S Project, normalizing named entities to unique geo-identifiers in Entity Linking, and exploring language understanding services in the ToHyVe Project. Akhyar's versatility and proficiency across diverse projects showcase his impressive ability to independently tackle complex problems. He primarily works within Docker containers to ensure reliable REST-API communication. As he nears completion of his Master's thesis, the team wishes him all the best for his future career endeavors."
- Dr. rer. nat. Philippe Thomas
